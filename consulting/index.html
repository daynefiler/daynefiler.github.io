The platform is now used across government, industry, and academia as the leading tool for processing and storing high-throughput and high-content chemical screening.

I am fluent with R, MySQL, and $x$ LaTeX, and can provide user-friendly analysis and automated reporting functionality to help you accomplish your goals.
