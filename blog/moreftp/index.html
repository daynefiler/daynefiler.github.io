Connecting to ftp servers

PUBLISHED ON FEB 20, 2020

I have again spent an absurd amount of time trying to connect to an ftp server, this time the Broad Institute server, with great difficulty.

Short story, start by entering ftp. Then, use the command open and type in the domain. You should then get a prompt for the username and password. I have found that trying ftp ftp.url often fails.
